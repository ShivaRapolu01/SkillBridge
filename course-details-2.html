About Instructor

Kartik Verma, an inter-IIT college-level squash player, is an excellent coach for junior players who are looking to develop their skills in the game. He has a strong foundation in teaching techniques and training methods for players of all ages. Kartik's coaching style is tailored to the needs of each player, taking into account their skill level and goals. He focuses on building a strong foundation of fundamental skills, such as proper technique and footwork, while also incorporating advanced strategies and tactics to help players improve their overall game.
